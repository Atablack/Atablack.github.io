Briefly about dreams

A dream is a succession of images, ideas, emotions and sensations that usually occur involuntarily in the mind during certain stages of sleep. Humans spend about two hours dreaming per night and each dream lasts around 5 to 20 minutes, although the dreamer may perceeive the dream as being much longer than this.
